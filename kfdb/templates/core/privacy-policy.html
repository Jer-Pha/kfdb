Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2024

Kinda Funny Database ("KFDB", "we", "our", "us") provides a comprehensive list of all YouTube and Patreon videos ("Content") by the company "Kinda Funny" for our visitors. KFDB is not endorsed by Kinda Funny and does not reflect the views or opinions of Kinda Funny or anyone officially involved in producing or managing Kinda Funny content.

Services

We provide users with the ability to view, filter, sort, and search through Kinda Funny content. Additionally, users are able to suggest edits to the displayed content if they believe something is missing or incorrect. Suggested edits must be approved by our content team before they are applied.

YouTube Content

Each piece of YouTube content includes the video's title, publish date, description and an embedded video to view directly on our site. This information is retrieved from the YouTube API. Additionally, our team has categorized each video into a "Channel" and "Show" for further filtering.

Patreon Content

All audio/video posts for the Membership tier (USD 10) at patreon.com/kindafunny are included in KFDB's content. Only the title and date of these posts are included on KFDB. The content of these posts is not part of KFDB's content, as they require paid membership on Patreon. Each post contains a link to Patreon.

Editing Content

Users are able to submit edits for content that they believe is missing or contains incorrect information. All data submitted as a "Suggestion", including the optional "Username", is saved internally by KFDB. This data is not shared with anyone.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2024

First-Party Cookies

We use two (2) "Strictly Necessary" cookies for KFDB. No personally-identifiable information is stored.

1) The "kfdb_session" cookie is linked to a user's session and ensures requests are genuine and prevents malicious activity from outside sources. This cookie expires at the end of each session.

2) The "kfdb_theme" cookie saves a user's choice for the site's color theme. This cookie expires after one (1) year.

Third-Party Cookies

KFDB does not use any third-party cookies. We use "youtube-nocookie.com" for all embedded YouTube videos. This prevents Google from adding cookies to users' browsers when viewing YouTube videos on KFDB. For more information, please visit Google Support > Manage video embedding options > Turn on privacy-enhanced mode.
